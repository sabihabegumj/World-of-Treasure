PROBABILITY

p(n)=Favourable outcome/Total outcome.
where p(n)-Probability of getting the event.
EG: Probability of getting head and tail= p(H & T)= (1/2)*(1/2) = 1/4
Probability of getting head or tail= p(H)+ p(T)= (1/2)+(1/2) = 1 which is a sure event.
